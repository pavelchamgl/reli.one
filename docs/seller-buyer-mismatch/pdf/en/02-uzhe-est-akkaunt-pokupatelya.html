Already Registered as a Buyer

What to do if you created a buyer account and now want to sell on Reli.one.

Version: June 2026

1. What the issue is

On Reli.one, buyer and seller accounts are different account types with different permissions. Buyer registration creates a user with the Customer role; to sell, you need the Seller role and completed onboarding (verification).

Important: the same email address cannot be used twice. If you already registered as a buyer, the form at https://reli.one/seller/create-account will return the error “user with this email already exists”.

2. What happens when you try to log in as a seller

Seller dashboard login is at https://reli.one/seller/login. Your buyer account password may work for authentication, but seller onboarding and seller features will not open until the account has the Seller role.

3. Options

Option A — Contact support (recommended)

Email office@reli.one with the subject “Convert account to seller” and include the email address you used to register as a buyer.

Email template:

Hello,

I registered on Reli.one as a buyer, but I would like to sell products on the platform.
Account email: your@email.com
Please help convert my account to a seller account or advise on next steps.

Best regards,
First Last

The support team will manually assign the seller role and create a Seller profile. After that, you can log in at https://reli.one/seller/login and complete onboarding with the same email.

Option B — Register a seller with a different email

Create a new seller account with a different email address:

  1. Open https://reli.one/seller/create-account;
  2. Register as a seller;
  3. Complete onboarding following the “New Seller Registration” guide.

Your buyer account on the old email remains — you can still place orders from it. Sales will be handled from the new seller account.

Option C — Delete the buyer account and register again

Warning: deleting your account will remove order history, favorites, and other data linked to your buyer profile. Use this option only if you have no active orders and are sure about your decision.
  1. Delete the account in profile settings (Delete account) or contact support;
  2. Wait for deletion confirmation;
  3. Register as a seller with the same email: https://reli.one/seller/create-account.

4. Comparing options

Option Same email Order history Speed
A — Support Yes Preserved 1–2 business days
B — New email No Preserved on old account Immediately
C — Delete + register Yes Lost Depends on deletion

5. After receiving the seller role

Regardless of the option you choose, you will need to:

  1. Log in at https://reli.one/seller/login;
  2. Complete onboarding (seller type → details → documents → submit application);
  3. Wait for approval;
  4. Start listing products (see the “Listing Products” PDF).
