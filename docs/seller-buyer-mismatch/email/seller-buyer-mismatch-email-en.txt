If you registered on Reli.one as a buyer but would like to sell products, below is a short guide. Three PDF attachments with step-by-step screenshots are included.

IMPORTANT: buyer and seller accounts are different account types. You cannot register as a seller with the same email if it is already used by a buyer account.

ATTACHMENTS:
1. 01-novaya-registraciya-prodavca-en.pdf — new seller registration
2. 02-uzhe-est-akkaunt-pokupatelya-en.pdf — if you already have a buyer account
3. 03-vykladka-tovarov-en.pdf — listing products after approval

QUICK START:
1. Register as a seller (or contact support to convert your account)
2. Confirm your email with the code from the message
3. Choose seller type: self-employed / sole proprietor or company
4. Fill in your details, bank details, addresses, and upload documents
5. Submit your application for review and wait for approval
6. Add products in the seller panel

LINKS:
• Seller registration: https://reli.one/seller/create-account
• Seller panel login: https://reli.one/seller/login

If your email is already registered as a buyer, email office@reli.one with the subject “Convert account to seller” — we will help.
