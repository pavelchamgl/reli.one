If you registered on Reli.one as a buyer but would like to sell products, below is a short guide. Three PDF attachments with detailed steps and screenshots are included.

Important: buyer and seller accounts are different account types. You cannot register as a seller with the same email if it is already used by a buyer account.

Three guides attached

1. New seller registration — if you have not registered yet or are ready to use a different email.
2. Already have a buyer account — what to do if your email is already taken.
3. Listing products — how to add a product after your seller application is approved.

Quick start

  1. Register as a seller (or contact support to convert your account).
  2. Confirm your email with the code from the message.
  3. Choose seller type: self-employed / sole proprietor or company.
  4. Fill in your details, bank details, addresses, and upload documents.
  5. Submit your application for review and wait for approval.
  6. Add products in the seller panel.
